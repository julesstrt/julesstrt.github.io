Training course with the **European Space Agency (ESA) Academy** on space debris and space sustainability.

**Topics covered:**
1. Forces acting on a space object
2. Space debris environment
3. The future of the environment
4. Mitigation principles and guidelines
5. Space debris mitigation context
6. Deorbiting and passivation
7. Space surveillance
8. Collision avoidance
9. Protection and shielding
10. Aerothermodynamics during re-entry
11. Spacecraft destruction during re-entry
12. On-ground risk estimation
13. Space debris mitigation technologies
14. Active debris removal
